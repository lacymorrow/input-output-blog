The Xspf Jukebox has received a new skin that resembles the Original Xspf Player. This skin has been recently posted on the project's website, providing users with more options to customize their experience. In addition to the new skin, the documentation for both the Gallery and the Jukebox has been updated. This ensures that users have access to accurate and up-to-date information when using these tools. With these updates, the Xspf Jukebox continues to provide a reliable and customizable music player solution.
