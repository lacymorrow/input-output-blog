The Jukebox is now able to be loaded as a child to any other flash movie, no problem. It can be included in Flash sites or applications without having to be loaded from the Html page. This update also fixes bugs with reading stats, and adds additional accessible information through the "statsurl" interface. [XSPF Jukebox](http://lacymorrow.com/projects/xspf)
